About

I work on understanding and exploiting frequency-domain failure modes of modern neural networks—especially in settings where high-frequency artifacts matter (deepfakes, generative forensics). My work combines theoretical ideas (spectral bias, inductive bias, optimization dynamics) with deployable systems and rigorous evaluation.
